About

My name is Dylan Mason. I'm a designer living in Calgary, Canada.

Work

During the day, I work on the mobile and web apps at Payload. We're bringing transparency and accountability to the transportation industry. Previously I worked at AppColony where we split our time between developing internal ideas and working with clients to make theirs a reality.

After hours, I'm one-half of Ritchie & Mason, a partnership with my friend Steven Ritchie. We come up with app ideas, throw many of them away and then choose to build a few of them.

Education

I attended the Alberta College of Art + Design where I studied graphic design for four fantastic years (along with printmaking, photography and illustration). After graduation, I wandered off to New York for a summer where I learned about Interaction Design at the School of Visual Arts.
